Antwerps Graffiti: The Laws / Rules

Author

Iida

Don’t snitch

Don’t write on religious buildings or war memorials

Don’t write on private homes

Don’t write on cars

Don’t go over someone who’s been in the game longer than you

If you are gonna go over someone, you need to do something better than they did. Otherwise, it’s going to look like a diss.

One of the biggest mistakes in the graffiti world: Painting over a dead writer’s work

Never paint over a tribute piece. Tribute pieces are pieces dedicated to dead graffiti writers.

Don’t ask to be put in crews

Learn your history. There’s nothing more toy than someone who paints but knows nothing about the history of graffiti.

Leave no trace like spray paint, spray caps or any other evidence.

Don’t act tough.
